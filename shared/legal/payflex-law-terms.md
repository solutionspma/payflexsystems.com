# PayFlex.Law - Program Terms

**Effective Date:** January 5, 2026

## 1. Service Definition

PayFlex.Law enables structured retainer payments and fee management.

**PayFlex does not provide legal services and is not a law firm.**

## 2. Funding Responsibility

Client is **solely responsible for funding** retainer programs.

**PayFlex.Law does not advance funds or assume financial risk.**

## 3. Trust Account Compliance

PayFlex.Law operates payment infrastructure. Trust accounting responsibilities remain with the law firm.

Programs must comply with all applicable bar association and state trust accounting rules.

## 4. Retainer Schedules

Retainer payment schedules are enforced automatically by the platform.

Law firms receive funds according to agreed schedules and retainer terms.

## 5. Branding

Programs may be client-branded; however, all materials must include **"Powered by PayFlex.Law"** where technically feasible.
